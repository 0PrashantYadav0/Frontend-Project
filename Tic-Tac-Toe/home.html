Tic Tac Toe Game

How to Play:

Tic Tac Toe is a two-player game where one player is "X" and the other is "O." The game is played on a 3x3 grid.

Players take turns placing their symbol on an empty cell. The first player to get three of their symbols in a row (horizontally, vertically, or diagonally) wins the game.


Winning Conditions:

You win the game when:

  • You have three of your symbols in a row horizontally.
  • You have three of your symbols in a row vertically.
  • You have three of your symbols in a row diagonally.
